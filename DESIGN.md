I have created the premium mobile home screen for Magic Ai Studio, designed to evoke a luxurious Korean wedding atelier atmosphere.
Design Highlights:
	•	Aesthetic & Palette: I used a soft blush pink and warm ivory background paired with a sophisticated wine rose accent for the primary buttons and the central navigation. This creates a romantic yet polished editorial look.
	•	Hero Experience: The "Before/After" hero card immediately showcases the service's value, using a realistic comparison between a studio portrait and a romantic Parisian snap.
	•	Typography: I chose an elegant serif for the headings to feel high-end and timeless, complemented by a clean sans-serif for UI elements to ensure mobile readability.
	•	Style Cards: Custom-generated visuals for the "Paris Eiffel Tower" and "Fairytale" concepts provide a concrete look at the transformations, presented in elegant, rounded cards with soft shadows.
	•	Intuitive Navigation: A floating bottom navigation bar centers the "Home" experience with a prominent action button, while the secondary "More Features" section remains light and uncluttered.
The design prioritizes whitespace and soft transitions to ensure it feels "emotional and warm" rather than clinical or tech-heavy.
